About InstaTruth

Our Mission

In an era where social media videos can reach millions within hours, the line between truth and misinformation has become increasingly blurred. At InstaTruth, we're on a mission to empower users with the tools they need to make informed decisions about the content they consume.

We believe that access to factual information is fundamental to a healthy digital ecosystem. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with rigorous verification methods to help users quickly determine the credibility of claims made in viral TikToks, Instagram Reels, and other social media videos.

Why We Built InstaTruth

Misinformation Crisis

Studies show that misinformation spreads 6x faster than factual content on social media, with video being the most effective medium.

Information Overload

The average person encounters hundreds of claims daily, making it impossible to manually verify everything they see and hear.

Empowerment Through AI

We believe technology that creates problems can also solve them, by automating verification and making fact-checking accessible to everyone.
