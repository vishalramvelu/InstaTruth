How InstaTruth Works

Our AI-powered platform analyzes social media videos to separate fact from fiction.

STEP 1

Audio Transcription & Analysis

When you submit a video link, our system downloads the content and uses an advanced audio model to transcribe any spoken content into text. This gives us an accurate record of all claims being made.

Our NLP models analyze the transcription for specific claims, statements of fact, and potentially misleading phrases, creating a structured dataset of verifiable points.

STEP 2

Source Verification

For each claim identified, our system conducts targeted web searches to find credible sources that either confirm or contradict the information. We prioritize authoritative sources like government websites, peer-reviewed journals, and established news outlets.

Our verification engine ranks sources by credibility and relevance, providing transparency about where information is coming from and how reliable it is.

STEP 3

Sentiment & Bias Analysis

Beyond fact-checking, we analyze the emotional tone and potential bias in the content. Our NLP models identify emotional manipulation tactics, loaded language, and framing techniques that might be used to mislead viewers.

This helps you understand not just if the information is true, but also how it's being presented and what psychological techniques may be at play.

STEP 4

Comprehensive Report

All analyses are compiled into a detailed, easy-to-understand report that shows you exactly what claims were made, which ones are supported by credible sources, and where the information might be misleading or incomplete.

Each report includes links to original sources, confidence scores for different claims, and an overall truth rating that helps you quickly gauge the reliability of the content.
